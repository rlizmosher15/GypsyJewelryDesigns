I am an independent jewelry designer located in Norfolk, VA. I began designing jewelry at the age of 10 with basic beading and grew my style over the years into the intricate wire weaving I design now. My company Gypsy Jewelry Designs has evolved from selling at simple fairs and conventions, to my Etsy store and creating custom commission pieces. I absolutely love what I do and am thrilled when people are excited to wear my creations!
